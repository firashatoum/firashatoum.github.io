About

Research profile without the noise.

I am trained in molecular and cell biology, with practical experience across wet-lab work and computational biology. My strongest interest is transcriptomic data analysis, especially workflows that move from raw sequencing data to interpretable biological results.

I care about clean project structure, reproducibility, transparent documentation, and figures that make the biology easier to understand. This website is a compact overview of selected work, technical skills, and research outputs.
